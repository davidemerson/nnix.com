# Bourbon Milk Punch

## Ingredients
* 2 oz bourbon
* 2 oz oat milk
* 0.5 t vanilla extract
* 0.5 oz 1:1 simple syrup
* ground nutmeg for garnish

## Procedure
* Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker, add ice, shake for 30sec. Strain into a glass filled with ice, sprinkle with nutmeg.
