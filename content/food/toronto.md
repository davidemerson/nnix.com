# Toronto

## Ingredients
* 2oz rye
* 0.25oz Fernet-Branca
* 0.25oz 2:1 simple syrup
* 2 dashes angostura bitters
* Orange peel for garnish

## Procedure
* Stir all ingredients over ice, strain into glass, garnish with orange peel.
